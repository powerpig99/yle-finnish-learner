Language Learning Subtitles

Learn languages by watching videos with immersive tools!
Supported: YLE Areena YouTube HTML5 Video
Watch videos with dual subtitles, click any word for instant translation, and control playback to study at your own pace.
Features:
  • Dual subtitles (original + your language)
  • Click any word for popup dictionary
  • Auto-pause after each subtitle (P key)
  • Skip subtitles with , and . keys
  • Playback speed control
  • Multiple translation providers (Google, DeepL, Claude, Gemini, Grok)
